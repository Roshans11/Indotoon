Help Center

Welcome to the Indotoon Help Center! Find answers to common questions below. If you need more assistance, feel free to contact us.

1. How do I create an account?

Click on the Sign Up button on the homepage, fill in your details, and follow the instructions to verify your email.

2. I forgot my password. What should I do?

Go to the login page and click on Forgot Password? Follow the instructions to reset your password via email.

3. How can I report a problem?

If you experience any technical issues or find inappropriate content, please email us at support@indotoon.com.

4. Is the content free to watch?

Yes! Indotoon provides free streaming of Indian anime content. However, some content may require you to sign in.

5. How can I join your community?

You can join our Telegram group, Discord server, or follow us on YouTube.

6. Need more help?

Email us at support@indotoon.com or use our contact form on the Contact page.
